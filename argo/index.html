Product Design

Originally, Fitt only posted articles online. Our goal was to take the articles people already loved and build valuable utilities on top of them.

We extracted places (i.e. gyms and restaurants) and events from articles and made them their own type of content. Using a card system within the original articles, we created a new layer where users could access additional details. This also allowed for place and event data to be presented in new, useful formats like maps and calendar feeds.

We also split the idea of articles into two formats with the addition of guides. Guides are list-style articles paired with maps that cover the best places a city has to offer on a particular subject like Yoga or Paleo-friendly Food.
