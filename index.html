Hi, I’m Kevin–
a digital designer living and working in Pittsburgh, PA.

I’m currently on the design team at Deeplocal where we invent products and experiences for brands that people love. Previously, I worked at MAYA—a studio that uses Human-Centered Design and an architectural approach to help companies big and small.

I’ve helped prototype and ship projects for a variety of clients including Google, Old Navy, Netflix, Nike, StoryCorps, Oreo, MSA, Highmark, and Amway. I can‘t show everything, but snippets of my more public work will live here.
