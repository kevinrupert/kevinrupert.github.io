About

I’m a multidisciplinary designer who specializes in experiences for every screen. Currently, I’m the Head of Design at Fitt—a website and app where you can find the best places to eat healthy, work out, run, and hike.

Previously, I was a designer at Deeplocal where I helped concept and execute experiences for brands like Netflix, Spotify, Google, and Nest.

Before that, I was designing, white-boarding, and post-it-noting on the team at MAYA.
